6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

• SSL/TLS encryption for all communications
• Data encryption at rest in Firebase Storage
• Secure authentication via OAuth 2.0
• Restricted access to personal data (principle of least privilege)
• Regular security audits
• Automatic backups
• DDoS attack protection
• Monitoring of unauthorized access

However, no system is 100% secure. We recommend using strong passwords and not sharing credentials.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for the following periods:

• Account data: while account remains active + 3 months
• Alerts and notifications: 24 months from sending
• Geolocation data: 12 months
• Multimedia content: 24 months or until you delete it
• Transaction data: 6 years (tax obligation)
• Access logs: 12 months (security)
• Report data: until resolution + 3 years

After these periods, data is securely and irreversibly deleted or anonymized.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a personal data subject, you have the right to:

• Access: obtain confirmation of what data we process and a copy thereof
• Rectification: correct inaccurate or incomplete data
• Erasure ("right to be forgotten"): request deletion of your data
• Restriction: request restriction of processing
• Portability: receive your data in structured format and transfer it to another controller
• Object: object to processing for legitimate reasons
• Withdraw consent: at any time, without retroactive effects
• Not be subject to automated decisions: including profiling

To exercise these rights:
1. Send request to: privacy@warnapp.com
2. Include copy of your ID
3. We will respond within a maximum of 1 month

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD) if you believe your rights are violated: www.aepd.es

9. Minors
